How to Multiply (Kid-Friendly!)

Multiplication means adding the same number over and over.

  • 3 × 4 means 3 groups of 4: 4 + 4 + 4 = 12.
  • Order doesn’t change the answer: 3 × 4 = 4 × 3 (commutative property).
  • Break apart to make it easier: 7 × 6 = 7 × (3 + 3) = 21 + 21 = 42 (associative/distributive idea).
  • Use skip-counting: 5s go 5, 10, 15, 20…

Tip: Look for patterns (like the 9s and 11s) to go faster!
